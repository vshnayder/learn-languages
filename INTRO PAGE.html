VIKTORIYA SHNAYDER
Jerusalem, ISRAEL

I was born in Kiev, Ukraine on July 1st, 1977.
I am Jewish/Ukrainian, and I immigrated to Israel on November 25th, 2002.
I currently live in Jerusalem.
I am fluent in Ukrainian, Russian and English.
I am still improving my Hebrew, Japanese and Spanish language skills.

My hobbies & interests are:

studying the Bible, listening to music, watching movies and news mostly on Youtube,
searching sites for new gadgets innovation, and learn new languages.
What I most enjoy is spending my time learning Japanese language.
I find it very interesting, challenging but mostly fun :).

Dear friends, visitors & members,
I created this site to help with learning a new language.
Having been a new immigrant myself, I know that language is like a bridge
to help people understand one another.
I am hoping to create fun ways to learn a language, and so
if you find it helpful for yourself, then please join me & let us learn together.
